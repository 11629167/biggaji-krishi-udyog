HR Sheets:

Typically, HR steel bars and plates feature slightly rounded edges and corners as well as modest distortions. There are some clear advantages over others, including its cold-rolled counterpart. Cold-rolled steel has certain distinct applications and it is favoured over hot-rolled steel in those scenarios.

Hot rolled (HR) sheets are a raw material used in many industries, including:

  • Construction: For bridges, infrastructure, and metal buildings.
  • Automotive: For door hinges, truck rims, and wheel discs.
  • Agriculture: For agricultural and mining equipment, and farm equipment components.
  • Machine structures: For welded pipes and tubes, and LPG cylinders.
  • Material handling: For forklift trucks, rail joint bars, and tram rails.
