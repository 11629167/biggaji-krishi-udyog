TMT BAR:

TMT bar is a type of reinforcement steel made by treating mild steel bars with heat and then rapidly cooling them to increase their strength and durability. These bars are used in various parts of the construction to provide longevity and strength to the structure. TMT bars are used in different construction types, areas and styles.

Advanced Features of Jindal TMT 550 Steel Bars:

  • Enhanced ductility combined with super strength.
  • Tremendous weldability without loss of strength in the welded places.
  • Better malleability and ductility.
  • Great savings in the cost of steel.
