Who We Are

Biggaji Krishi Udyog was founded in 2017 in Lalgarh, Churu, Rajasthan, by Mr. Kailash Chand. The company specializes in providing high-quality materials and equipment for the agricultural industry. They offer products like iron rods, HR sheets, and agricultural machinery, catering to both wholesale and retail markets. Our Vision and Mission

Our Vision

To be a trusted partner in the agricultural sector by providing top-quality tools and materials that help farmers achieve their goals while promoting sustainable farming practices.

Our Mission
