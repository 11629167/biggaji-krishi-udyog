Welcome to Biggaji Krishi Udyog

Founded in 2017, Biggaji Krishi Udyog is a leading supplier of agricultural tools, iron rods, HR sheets, and farming equipment. Based in Lalgarh, Churu, Rajasthan, we are committed to providing high-quality products to enhance the efficiency and productivity of farmers. Our mission is to support the agricultural industry with innovative solutions and sustainable practices.

High-Quality Agricultural Tools

We provide reliable tools and equipment to ensure efficient farming operations. From plows to harvesters, we have everything you need.

Iron Rods & HR Sheets

Our durable iron rods and HR sheets are ideal for construction and agricultural infrastructure. Strong, flexible, and affordable materials for your needs.

Farming Machinery

Increase your productivity with our range of advanced farming machinery. From tractors to modern harvesting machines, we offer machines that get the job done faster and easier.
